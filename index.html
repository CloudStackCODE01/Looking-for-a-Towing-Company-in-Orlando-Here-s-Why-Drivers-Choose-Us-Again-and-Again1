What to Do When You Need a Towing Company in Orlando

If you find yourself needing a tow, don’t panic. Follow these steps:

  1. Stay Safe: If possible, move your vehicle to a safe location. Turn on your hazard lights.
  2. Call Us: Reach out to Javi’s Towing and Transport Orlando at https://javistowing.com. Provide your location and a brief description of your issue.
  3. Wait in a Safe Spot: Stay inside your vehicle if it’s safe to do so, or find a nearby place away from traffic.
  4. Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask about estimated arrival time or service options. We’re always happy to help clarify.
  5. Have Your Info Ready: If you’re calling for insurance reimbursement later, take notes about the time of service, driver name, and vehicle condition.
